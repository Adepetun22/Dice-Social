Create Your Business
Go to your BCC dashboard and click Add New Business.
  • Choose Service as your business category type.
  • Then select Business Representative as your business category.
  • Fill in the other required business details and submit.
Get Approved and Access Your Dashboard
Once your application is approved, you’ll receive an email and see a new Business Rep tab on your side menu. From there, you can explore all the tools and features available to you.
